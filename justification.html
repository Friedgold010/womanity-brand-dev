Design justifcation Final design choices were based on the initial user testing goals whihc were ease of use and a universal look for the whole brand website without making the different pages too confusing. From the clear white navbar with navigation links we made sure it was minimalistic yet clear. Next we used the brand colors provided in the Brand road map to add a mixe of different colors to the background so it would just blend in. The fonts and colors were used to convey a sense of minimalism and professionalism. The language was to the point and educational speaking to our consumers in a educational yet directive tone. To make sure our messages, values and vision as a company was set in stone. Final website tested once more to make sure it contained all the right design elements like minimalism, ease of use and clear navigational choices with a universal design in color and looks. Fonts used were: Bebas Neue BOLD, Roboto BOLD and Railway. Colors used were the same as shown in the brand road map which are: #b17463, #d59d8e, #777, #000
